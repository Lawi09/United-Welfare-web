Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. How can I become a member of UWO?

You can join by filling out the membership form on our Application page and submitting it for approval.

2. How do I make payments or contributions?

Payments are made through our Payment page using M-Pesa. You’ll receive a confirmation and our treasurer will be notified automatically.

3. What is the UWO Chama?

It’s our community savings and welfare group where members contribute regularly, access soft loans, and receive support during emergencies or development projects.

4. How can I get assistance or report an issue?

You can reach us directly through the contacts provided below for organization or software-related support.
