Who We Are

United Welfare Organization (UWO) is a community-based initiative dedicated to improving the quality of life of individuals and families through empowerment, education, and social support programs. We aim to build a society where everyone has equal opportunities to grow and succeed.

Our History

UWO began as a small neighborhood welfare group founded by community members who recognized the need for collective growth and structured support systems. Through transparency, teamwork, and leadership, it has grown into a recognized organization impacting lives through welfare activities, training, and empowerment programs.

Our Mission

To empower individuals and communities to realize their full potential by promoting social welfare, unity, and self-reliance through education, capacity building, and sustainable initiatives.

Our Vision

A united, empowered, and self-sustaining community where every person enjoys dignity, opportunity, and equality.

Our Objectives

  • Promote unity, peace, and cooperation among members.
  • Support education, health, and environmental initiatives.
  • Empower youth and women through skill development and entrepreneurship.
  • Provide social and financial support to members in need.
  • Collaborate with partners and institutions for shared community growth.

Our Savings and Welfare Chama

United Welfare Organization operates a community chama (savings group) to encourage financial discipline, investment, and mutual support among members. Through the chama, members make regular contributions which are used for welfare activities, soft loans, and emergency assistance.

The chama also funds small business ventures and community projects, helping members improve their livelihoods while promoting trust and cooperation. All financial activities are managed transparently by elected officials to ensure accountability and fairness.

Benefits of the UWO Chama

  • Encourages a savings culture among members.
  • Provides access to low-interest loans and emergency support.
  • Funds income-generating and community development projects.
  • Promotes unity and cooperation through shared responsibility.
  • Supports members during personal or family challenges.

What We Do

  • Organize welfare drives and outreach programs.
  • Support education sponsorship and mentorship for students.
  • Conduct environmental awareness and clean-up activities.
  • Provide entrepreneurship and financial literacy training.
  • Offer counseling and guidance to members in need.
